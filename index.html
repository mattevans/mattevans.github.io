Deutsche Telekom

Sea Hero Quest is a multi-platform mobile game designed specifically to help advance the understanding of spatial navigation, and therefore understand one of the first symptoms of dementia.

Downloads of the game since its global launch have tipped 2.5 million from a total of 193 countries. Game play has generated the equivalent of more than 9,400 years of similar lab based research. The result: the world's largest baseline study yielding important global baseline data for dementia research.

The app was been awarded with 9 Cannes Lions Award in 2016.

  • Client: Deutsche Telekom | Sea Hero Quest
  • Completed at: Saatchi & Saatchi

Mattessons

FRHANK, the world's first artificially intelligent snacking and gaming robot co-created by a social community, was developed to promote Mattessons Fridge Raiders snacks. The web application was developed as a way for users to interact with the robot (FRHANK). The system is designed to populate the brain of the robot with user submitted intelligence and imagery which is then dictacted or displayed on the robot himself.

  • Client: Mattessons — Fridge Raiders
  • Completed at: Saatchi & Saatchi
