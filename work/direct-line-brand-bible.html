DirectLine – Brand Bible


Direct Line had just undergone a huge brand transformation with the need arising to create a new brand positioning, identity and guidelines. This 'Brand Bible' was developed to engage and inspire staff as well as set the new boundaries to align all creative work going forward. The bible is an online platform developed to host all the brand's internal information, guidelines, history, awards, etc.
