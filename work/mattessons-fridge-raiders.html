Work
MATTESSONS FRIDGE RAIDERS


FRHANK, the world's first artificially intelligent snacking and gaming robot co-created by a social community, was developed to promote Mattessons Fridge Raiders snacks. The web application was developed as a way for users to interact with the robot (FRHANK). The system is designed to populate the brain of the robot with user submitted intelligence and imagery which is then dictacted or displayed on the robot himself.

  • Client: Mattessons — Fridge Raiders
  • Completed at: Saatchi & Saatchi
